The Story

The Love Drops is a parody singalong band. We take songs you already know and rewrite the words — sometimes for a specific event, sometimes because we got hooked on a theme.

The whole thing is built around one idea: everyone's in the band. There's no "audience". We bring the music and the chord sheets, you bring your voice. We don't care if you think you're singing out of tune — it sounds great to us.

"If you're hitting every note, you're not really singing along."

— The Love Drops

We sometimes play parties and festivals. We've never used AI to help write our songs; if we ever do, we'll say so on the song itself.

It's an open project. If you like a song, take it and use it. Our material is shared under a Creative Commons BY-NC-SA 4.0 license — share freely, remix freely, no commercial use, attribution to The Love Drops. If you do something fun with one, we'd love to hear about it.

What We Believe

Perfection is Boring

Just a room full of people singing. Some of them have instruments. All of them are in the band.
